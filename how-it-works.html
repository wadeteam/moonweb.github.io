How It Works

How to earn on our platform?



1 - Invite MOON

You can invite MOON to your own server or join some servers that already added it! In case you only need to  try it, you can join Our support server. 


2 - Create new wallet

Use command !new and wait few seconds. You can have only 1 wallet. Now it is time to begin your crypto
journey!


3 - Earn some $MOON

Use command !earn, !faucet, !shortlnks or !learn to earn $MOON. You can also earn some $MOON with our
partners!


4 - Exchange $MOON

Join our community server, open #Exchange channel and trade some $MOON to BTC. Please note that this
function is under developement :)


5- Withdraw BTC

Connect your LN URL with MOON (!wallet > connections > connect BTC > send your LNURL) And withdraw!
